A Letter For You

My love,

On this beautiful day that the universe gifted you life, I find myself overwhelmed with a gratitude I can barely put into words. You exist, and that alone has changed my world in ways I never saw coming.

"Even when things become difficult, my heart still finds home in you." I want you to know that. Not as a phrase, but as a truth I return to every single day.

I will not pretend our journey has been without turbulence, I just hope it will be calm soon 💔. We have had our storms, moments of misunderstanding, moments where the distance felt heavier than it should have, moments where I know I fell short. I carry those moments not with guilt, but with the quiet commitment to do better, to love more carefully, and to choose you more intentionally.

"Distance may separate our hands, but never our souls 🥹." Across every mile between us, there is a thread, invisible but unbreakable that will always hold. I feel it when I think of you. I feel it when I miss you. I feel it especially today.

More than candles and celebrations, what I truly wish for you today is peace. Peace in your mind. Softness in your heart. The kind of quiet joy that settles deep and stays. "More than anything, I just want you to be happy, peaceful, and feel loved."

I want to celebrate many more birthdays with you, through html code for now😂, and someday in the same room, in the same laughter, in the same air. "I still look forward to every future birthday beside you."

You are not just someone I love. You are someone I deeply admire. Your strength in difficult seasons. Your warmth in ordinary moments. The way you carry yourself. The realness of you... all of it.

So today, on your birthday, let this be your reminder: You are loved. You are seen. You are cherished in ways that distance cannot diminish and words cannot fully capture.

Happy Birthday, my love. This one's for you. 🌹

Forever yours 🫂❤️
